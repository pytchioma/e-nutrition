Our Story

Born to Boost

We started with one question: why can't an energy drink actually be good for you?

How It Started

From a Kitchen Experiment to 50,000 Fans

In 2021, our founder Ngozi was tired of energy drinks that left her jittery, crashed her mood, and tasted like chemicals. So she started blending her own in her kitchen — real fruit, clean caffeine, and a touch of magic.

After months of testing and hundreds of taste tests with friends, Strawberry Bliss was born. The response was overwhelming. Within a year, Boost had grown from a kitchen project to a brand loved by athletes, creatives, and everyday people who just want to feel their best.
